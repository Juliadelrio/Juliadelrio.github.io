In "Mice" she uses wireless computer mice, turning them into (electromagnetic) musical instruments. Beats and drones from the communication between mice and computer reveals the differences between the design of each model. The possibilities of interacting with those objects changes, the hand touches the mouse with a different purpose, experimenting with unsual positions and interactions in order to explore the sonical posiblities offered by this altered interface.
